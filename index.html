8123 means everything to me.
-We All Roll Along, The Maine
If there is one thing I can talk at length about with little to no preparation, it's The Maine. I have been in love with this band for the last ten years. The Maine is a rock band formed in 2007 and based out of Tempe, AZ. Their most recent album, released March 29, 2019, is called You Are OK. I cannot recommend listening to this album enough.
